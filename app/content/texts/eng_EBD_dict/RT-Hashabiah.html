HASHABIAH

"HASHABIAH "regarded by Jehovah. (1.) Merarite Levite (1 Chronicles 6:45; 9:14). (2.) A son of Jeduthun (25:3, 19). (3.) Son of Kemuel (26:30).
(4.) One of the chief Levites (2 Chronicles 35:9). (5.) A Levite (Nehemiah 11:22). (6.) One of the chief priests in the time of Ezra (Ezra 8:24). (7.) A chief of the Levites (Nehemiah 12:24). (8.) Ezra 8:19. (9.) Nehemiah 3:17.
