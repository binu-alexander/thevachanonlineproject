REFUGE

"REFUGE", CITIES OF were six in number (Numbers 35). 1. On the west of Jordan were (1) Kadesh, in Naphtali; (2) Shechem, in Mount Ephraim;(3) Hebron, in Judah. 2. On the east of Jordan were, (1) Golan, in Bashan;(2) Ramoth-Gilead, in Gad; and (3) Bezer, in Reuben. (See under each of these names.)
