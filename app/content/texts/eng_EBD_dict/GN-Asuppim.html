ASUPPIM

"ASUPPIM "(1 Chronicles 26:15, 17, Authorized Version; but in Revised Version, “storehouse”), properly the house of stores for the priests. In Nehemiah 12:25 the Authorized Version has “thresholds,” marg.
“treasuries” or “assemblies;” Revised Version, “storehouses.”
