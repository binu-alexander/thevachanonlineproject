COAT

"COAT "the tunic worn like the shirt next the skin (Leviticus 16:4; Cant.
5:3; 2 Samuel 15:32; Exodus 28:4; 29:5). The “coats of skins” prepared by God for Adam and Eve were probably nothing more than aprons (Genesis 3:21). This tunic was sometimes woven entire without a seam (John 19:23); it was also sometimes of “many colours” (Genesis 37:3; R.V.
marg., “a long garment with sleeves”). The “fisher’s coat” of John 21:7 was obviously an outer garment or cloak, as was also the “coat” made by Hannah for Samuel (1 Samuel 2:19). (See DRESS.)
