EAST WIND

"EAST WIND "the wind coming from the east (Job 27:21; Isaiah 27:8, etc.).
Blight caused by this wind, “thin ears” (Genesis 41:6); the withered“gourd” (Jonah 4: 8). It was the cause and also the emblem of evil (Ezekiel 17:10; 19:12; Hos. 13:15). In Palestine this wind blows from a burning desert, and hence is destitute of moisture necessary for vegetation.
