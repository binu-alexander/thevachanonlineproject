SEBA

"SEBA "(1.) One of the sons of Cush (Genesis 10:7).
(2.) The name of a country and nation (Isaiah 43:3; 45:14) mentioned along with Egypt and Ethiopia, and therefore probably in north-eastern Africa.
The ancient name of Meroe. The kings of Sheba and Seba are mentioned together in Psalm 72:10.
