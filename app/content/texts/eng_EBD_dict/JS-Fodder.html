FODDER

"FODDER" Hebrews belil, (Job 6:5), meaning properly a mixture or medley(Lat. farrago), “made up of various kinds of grain, as wheat, barley, vetches, and the like, all mixed together, and then sown or given to cattle”(Job 24:6, A.V. “corn,” R.V. “provender;” Isaiah 30:24, provender”).
