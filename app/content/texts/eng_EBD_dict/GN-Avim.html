AVIM

"AVIM "a people dwelling in Hazerim, or “the villages” or “encampments” on the south-west corner of the sea-coast (Deuteronomy 2:23). They were subdued and driven northward by the Caphtorim. A trace of them is afterwards found in Joshua 13:3, where they are called Avites.
