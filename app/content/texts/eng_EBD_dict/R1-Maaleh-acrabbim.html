MAALEH-ACRABBIM

"MAALEH-ACRABBIM "ascent of the scorpions; i.e., “scorpion-hill”, a pass on the south-eastern border of Palestine (Numbers 34:4; Joshua 15:3). It is identified with the pass of Sufah, entering Palestine from the great Wady el-Fikreh, south of the Dead Sea. (See AKRABBIM.)
