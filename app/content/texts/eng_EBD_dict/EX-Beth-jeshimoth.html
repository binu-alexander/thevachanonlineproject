BETH-JESHIMOTH

"BETH-JESHIMOTH "house of wastes, or deserts, a town near Abel-shittim, east of Jordan, in the desert of Moab, where the Israelites encamped not long before crossing the Jordan (Numbers 33:49; A.V.,“Bethjesimoth”). It was within the territory of Sihon, king of the Amorites(Joshua 12:3).
