BOND

"BOND "an obligation of any kind (Numbers 30:2, 4, 12). The word means also oppression or affliction (Psalm 116:16; Phil. 1:7). Christian love is the“bond of perfectness” (Colossians 3:14), and the influences of the Spirit are the “bond of peace” (Ephesians 4:3).
