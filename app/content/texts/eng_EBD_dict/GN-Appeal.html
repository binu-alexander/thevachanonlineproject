APPEAL

"APPEAL "a reference of any case from an inferior to a superior court.
Moses established in the wilderness a series of judicatories such that appeals could be made from a lower to a higher (Exodus 18:13-26.) Under the Roman law the most remarkable case of appeal is that of Paul from the tribunal of Festus at Caesarea to that of the emperor at Rome(Acts 25:11, 12, 21, 25). Paul availed himself of the privilege of a Roman citizen in this matter.
