GIBEAH-HAARALOTH

"GIBEAH-HAARALOTH "(Joshua 5:3, marg.), hill of the foreskins, a place at Gilgal where those who had been born in the wilderness were circumcised. All the others, i.e., those who were under twenty years old at the time of the sentence at Kadesh, had already been circumcised.
