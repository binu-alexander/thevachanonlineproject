MOSERA

"MOSERA "a bond, one of the stations of the Israelites in the wilderness(Deuteronomy 10:6), at the foot of Mount Hor. (Comp. Numbers 33:37, 38). It has been identified with el-Tayibeh, a small fountain at the bottom of the pass leading to the ascent of Mount Hor.
