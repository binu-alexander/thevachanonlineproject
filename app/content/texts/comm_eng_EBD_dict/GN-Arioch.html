ARIOCH

"ARIOCH "lion-like, venerable. (1.) A king of Ellasar who was confederate with Chedorlamer (Genesis 14:1,9). The tablets recently discovered by Mr. Pinches (see CHALDEA) show the true reading is Eri-Aku of Larsa.
This Elamite name meant “servant of the moon-God.” It was afterwards changed into Rimsin, “Have mercy, O moon-God.” (2.) Daniel 2:14.
