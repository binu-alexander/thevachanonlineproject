PLOUGH

"PLOUGH "first referred to in Genesis 45:6, where the Authorized Version has “earing,” but the Revised Version “ploughing;” next in Exodus 34:21 and Deuteronomy 21:4. The plough was originally drawn by oxen, but sometimes also by asses and by men. (See AGRICULTURE.)
