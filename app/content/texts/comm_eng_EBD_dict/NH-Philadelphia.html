PHILADELPHIA

"PHILADELPHIA "brotherly love, a city of Lydia in Asia Minor, about 25 miles south-east of Sardis. It was the seat of one of the “seven churches”(Revelation 3:7-12). It came into the possession of the Turks in A.D.
1392. It has several times been nearly destroyed by earthquakes. It is still a town of considerable size, called Allahshehr, “the city of God.”
