BERACHAH

"BERACHAH "blessing. (1.) A valley not far from Engedi, where Jehoshaphat overthrew the Moabites and Ammonites (2 Chronicles 20:26). It has been identified with the valley of Bereikut. (R.V.,“Beracah.”)(2.) One of the Benjamite warriors, Saul’s brethren, who joined David when at Ziklag (1 Chronicles 12:3).
