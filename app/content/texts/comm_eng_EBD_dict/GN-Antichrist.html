ANTICHRIST

"ANTICHRIST "against Christ, or an opposition Christ, a rival Christ. The word is used only by the apostle John. Referring to false teachers, he says(1 John 2:18, 22; 4:3; 2 John 1:7), “Even now are there many antichrists.”(1.) This name has been applied to the “little horn” of the “king of fierce countenance” (Daniel 7:24, 25; 8:23-25).
(2.) It has been applied also to the “false Christs” spoken of by our Lord(Matthew 24:5, 23, 24).
(3.) To the “man of sin” described by Paul (2 Thessalonians 2:3, 4, 8-10).
(4.) And to the “beast from the sea” (Revelation 13:1; 17:1-18).
