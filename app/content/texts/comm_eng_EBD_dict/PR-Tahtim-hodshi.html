TAHTIM-HODSHI

"TAHTIM-HODSHI "the land of the newly inhabited, (2 Samuel 24:6). It is conjectured that, instead of this word, the reading should be, “the Hittites of Kadesh,” the Hittite capital, on the Orontes. It was apparently some region east of the Jordan and north of Gilead.
