BRIDE

"BRIDE "frequently used in the ordinary sense (Isaiah 49:18; 61:10, etc.).
The relation between Christ and his church is set forth under the figure of that between a bridegroom and bride (John 3:29). The church is called “the bride” (Revelation 21:9; 22:17). Compare parable of the Ten Virgins(Matthew 25:1-13).
